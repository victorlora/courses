To begin the simulation you will be asked to select a car model, a car color, the engine in the car, and a track to race on. Your goal is to beat your opponent (the computer). Certain predetermined combinations of user inputs will be more likely to win, and by running the simulation multiple times you will be able to determine which combination leads to the fastest time.

Good Luck!
